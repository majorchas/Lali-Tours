Summer season package

10 Nights and 11 day package

  • 1st Day

    Airport to Negambo

    Check into Hotel and relax,afternoon visit Buddhist Temple, local market area, fisherman beach and their activities, back to hotel for the overnight stay

  • 2nd Day

    Negambo to Weligama

    After the breakfast proceed to Colombo do the little city tour and proceed to Weligama En-Route visit Turtle hatchery and do the beautiful Madu river safari famous Dutch fort in galle, stick fisherman and proceed to hotel.

  • 3rd Day

    Weligama to Thissamaharama

    After the breakfast leave the hotel and proceed to Thissamaharama. En-route visiting down south coastal belt , check into the hotel and afternoon do the Yala wild life safari, back to the hotel.

  • 4th Day

    Thissamaharama to Ella

    After the breakfast proceed to Ella,en-route visit Katharagama Sanctuary,ancient temple at Buduruwagala, Rawana falls and check in to the hotel.afternoon city tour and you can do a trekking to mini Adam's peak , famous nine arch bridge and back to the hotel.

  • 5th Day

    Kandy to Ella

    if you like you can travel by train from ella to Nuwara Eliya visiting beautiful panorama do the little city tour in Nuwara Eliya and proceed to Kandy. en-route visiting beautiful Tea plantation and Tea factory.Check in to the hotel afternoon visit cultural dance and Temple of the tooth relic and back to the hotel.

  • 6th Day

    Kandy to Habarana

    after the breakfast proceed to Sigiriya en-route visit beautiful hindu temple and if you desired you can visit Herbal and Spice garden at Matale. ancient natural cave temple at dambulla(1st centuary B.C). drive to Sigiriya and climb the marvalous rock fortress (5st centuary A.D) check in to the hotel.

  • 7th Day

    Habarana to Anuradhapura

    After the breakfast in the morning visit 2nd ancient capital Polonnaruwa and proceed to Anuradhapura. afternoon visit ancient city of Anuradhapura check in to the hotel.

  • 8th Day

    Anuradhapura to Trincomalee

    after the breakfast on the way visit Mihintale ancient rock temple and proceed to Trincomalee beach.

  • 9th Day and 10th Day

    Leissure at the beach

    Two days at the beach

  • 11th Day

    Trincomalee to Airport

    Proceed to the airport according to flight time.
