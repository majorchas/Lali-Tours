Winter season package

9 Nights and 10 day package

  • 1st Day

    Airport to Negambo

    Check into Hotel and relax,afternoon visit Buddhist Temple, local market area, fisherman beach and their activities, back to hotel for the overnight stay

  • 2nd Day

    Negambo to Anuradhapura

    After the breakfast proceed to Sigiriya visiting rock fortress of Sigiriya built (5th centuary A.D) proceed to Anuradhapura check in to the hotel.

  • 3rd Day

    Anuradhapura to Polonnaruwa

    After the breakfast visit Anuradhapura.it was first capital of Sri Lanka build (4th centuary B.C) Here you can see lot of ruins and biggest stupa and oldest Bo tree in the world and proceed to Mihintale.That is a largest Natural rock temple and the cradle of Buddhism in Sri Lanka. (3rd centuary B.C) proceed to Polonnaruwa visit museum Royal palace and can see lot of ruins belongs to (11th and 12th centuary A.D) and proceed hotel for the overnight stay.

  • 4th Day

    Polonnaruwa to Kandy

    Route visit very beautiful and longest natural cave temple at dambulla (1st centuary B.C) and after if you desire you can visit a Herbal and Spice garden at Matale and highest hindu temple in Sri Lanka. then proceed to Kandy visit cultural dance and temple of the tooth relic and check in to the hotel for the overnight stay.

  • 5th Day

    Kandy to Ella

    After breakfast leave the hotel and proceed to Nuwara Eliya visiting tea plantation, waterfalls and tea factory and little city tour. proceed to the station at Nanuoya to do the beautiful sightseeing tour by the train from Nanuoya to Ella and check in to the hotel for the over night stay.

  • 6th Day

    Ella to Thissamaharama

    After breakfast leave the hotel and you can do a trekking to mini Adams peak and the famous Nine Arch Bridge and enjoy the village atmosphere ad you can view the Rawana fall and ancient Buduruwagala temple and proceed to Thissamaharama in the afternoon do the wild life safari and check into the hotel.

  • 7th Day

    Thissamaharama to Weligama
    After the breakfast visit Katharagama Sanctuary and proceed to Weligama for the beach stay.

  • 8th Day and 9th Day

    Leissure at the Beach
    Two days enjoy at the beach

  • 10th Day

    Galle to Airport
    Visiting Famous Dutch Fortin Galle. proceed to the Airport according to the flight time.

First day and Last day of the program may be little bit changed.
